Featured Project:
Theremin Painter

A theremin is an electronic musical instrument that allows its musician to manipulate volume and pitch by moving their hands. As the mouse moves from left to right, the frequency of the sound and the frequency of the painter both increase. As the mouse moves from bottom to top, the volume of the sound and the size of the painter both increase. Dip the mouse into the color palette to change the painter color.

About

Ryan Steed is a software engineer, quantitative model designer, subject matter analyst, and student. He is a software developer at the Laboratory for Unconventional Conflict Analysis and Simulation (LUCAS) in Duke University's Social Science Research Institute. He graduated from the North Carolina School of Science and Mathematics in 2017 and is currently studying Economics and Computer Science at George Washington University.
